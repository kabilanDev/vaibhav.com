Turmeric powder1/4 Lb
Kumkum1 Packet
Sandalwoodpaste / Powder1 Packet

Camphor1 Packet
Betel Leaves and NutsApprox. 30
Flowers4 bunches
Fruits12 Bananas and 5 other Fruits
Coconuts4
Rice5 Lbs
Kalasa Vasthram1 Towel or 2 Yards Fabric

coins ( quarters )20
